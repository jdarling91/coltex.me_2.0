Welcome! My name is Jonathan Darling and I am a senior at the University of Texas at Dallas studying Softare Engineering. I am also an intern at AllPlayers.com where I do everything from mobile app development to QA testing. My passion in development is frontend development on mobile devices and on the web, though I definitely don't mind getting in behind the scenes. Outside of development, my hobbies and interests include music, computer hardware, video games, and cars.

AllPlayers.com Android Application

The AllPlayers.com Android Application was one of the first major assignments given to me at AllPlayers.com. Myself and one other intern were given a barebones application that performed only basic functionality. Our goal was to bring this application up to date so that it would become a more valuable tool for our members to use. Having no Android development experience at all, this proved to be an excellent learning experience for my colleague and myself. I personally was responsible for parts of the groups interface and application-wide navigation interface, and the entirety of the maps, messaging, event creation, and account creation interfaces. I was also personally responsible for testing of the application and releasing a beta of the application to the Google Play Store.
